---

## Design Tips for Canva

1. **Keep center 60% clear** - that's where numbers go
2. **Darker edges, lighter center** - makes text pop
3. **Subtle patterns only** - don't overwhelm
4. **Professional look** - this represents your brand
5. **Test at small size** - will it look good as thumbnail?

## Quick Win Option

If Canva AI doesn't work well, just create:
- Solid gradient from dark green (#14532d) to darker slate (#0f172a)
- Solid gradient from dark red (#7f1d1d) to darker slate (#0f172a)

Simple gradients often look better than AI-generated complexity!
